📋 Penalty Rules

Guard absent — per day
BDT 640
Per absent guard per day
Applied when vendor marks a guard absent and no replacement is provided within 2 hours of shift start.
SLA breach — per hour
BDT 1,250
Per hour beyond SLA
Applied when a service ticket exceeds its SLA deadline. Calculated from SLA breach time to resolution time.
Late arrival — guard
BDT 320
Per occurrence
Applied when a guard arrives more than 15 minutes late to their shift without prior notification.
Vehicle not on time
BDT 2,500
Per occurrence
Applied when fleet vendor fails to deliver vehicle within 30 minutes of requested time causing operational impact.
All rules are defined in vendor contracts · Changes require FM Manager approval
